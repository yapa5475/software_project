Live Tutoring

Live tutoring gives you the opportunity to immediately connect with tutors near you to recieve quick help on homework and studying. Simply choose your courses and create an appointment with the scheduler. For more information, contact: livetutoring@gmail.com.

"Why Us"

Not just any tutor can become a Live tutor. All of our tutors are selected based on there evaluation about there academic achievements and also how well are they good with their teaching abilities. At the end of every session, we highly recommend our students to give an evaluation about our tutors as it will help us maintain only the best tutors on our roster. We typically match our students withn 15 minutes and once you are matched with the tutor, you will then be able to recieve your LiveTutoring tutor details.

"Satifaction"

We are not happy, until you are happy. You are always welcome to try different tutors until you find a tutor who works for you the best.
